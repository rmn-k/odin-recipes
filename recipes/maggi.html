Maggi

Description

Maggi is an instant noodle which you can make very easily at home.
It is loved by many!

Ingredients

  • Maggi packet
  • 2 glasses of water

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Take out noodles from the packet and pour it in
  3. ALso add the spices from the sachet.
  4. Let it be as the water evaporates.
